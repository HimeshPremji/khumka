Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about choosing, caring for, and styling our earrings.

People ask this question often: Generally, you should choose earrings that complement your face shape. For a round face, choose long, dangling earrings to elongate your face. If you have a square face, choose earrings with soft, curved lines to soften your features. For size, pick earrings that are proportionate to your face and won’t overwhelm your features.

Popular earring styles include: Studs, hoops, drop earrings, chandeliers, and huggies. Studs offer a simple, classic look, while hoops add a bold statement. Drops and chandeliers bring elegance to any outfit, and huggies offer a snug, stylish vibe.

To keep your earrings looking their best: Store them in a jewelry box or pouch, avoid wearing them in water (like in the shower or pool), and clean them regularly with a soft cloth to maintain their shine and quality.

Common high-quality materials include: Gold, silver, platinum, and hypoallergenic stainless steel. For stones, diamonds, pearls, and premium crystals are often used, adding elegance and durability to earrings.

Pappu Jhumkas

Jhumkas are a traditional Indian earring style that has been adored by women for generations. These earrings dangle bell-like below the earlobe, creating a mesmerizing effect that is both elegant and captivating. At Kushals, we offer a range of Jhumkas made with exquisite materials like gold, pearl, and crystal.
Traditionally, Jhumkas are worn with Indian attire, but they can also be paired with Western outfits for a fusion effect that is both unique and stylish. Our collection of Jhumkas is designed to cater to every occasion, from simple and elegant designs for everyday wear to statement pieces that are perfect for weddings and other special events.
Our skilled artisans meticulously craft each pair of Jhumkas, ensuring that every detail is perfect. From the intricate patterns to the delicate workmanship, our Jhumkas are a testament to the artistry and craftsmanship of Indian jewellery making.
So whether you're looking to add a hint of elegance to your traditional attire or a fusion twist to your Western outfits, our collection of Jhumkas has something for everyone. Shop now and add a timeless piece of Indian jewellery to your collection!
